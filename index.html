About Peptide Pilot

Peptide Pilot helps you stay organized and accurate while managing peptide protocols. Everything is stored privately on your device — there is no account to create and no data is sent to our servers.

  • Reconstitution calculator with a visual syringe guide
  • Vial inventory with concentration, remaining amount, and expiry tracking
  • Dose logbook with protocols and reminders
  • Weight, symptom, and progress-photo tracking
  • Injection-site rotation guidance
  • JSON backup & restore and CSV data export

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a peptide / vial?
  1. Open the Vials tab.
  2. Tap the + button in the top corner.
  3. Enter the peptide name, total amount (mg), and the volume of bacteriostatic water you'll use to reconstitute it.
  4. Tap Save. Peptide Pilot calculates the concentration automatically, and the vial appears in your inventory and the reconstitution calculator.
How do I back up my data?
  1. Go to Settings → Backup & Restore.
  2. Tap Export Backup. This creates a single .json file containing all of your vials, protocols, doses, and logs.
  3. Use the share sheet to save it to Files, iCloud Drive, AirDrop, or email it to yourself.

We recommend exporting a backup periodically and before updating or reinstalling the app, since all data is stored locally on your device.

How do I restore my data from a backup?
  1. Go to Settings → Backup & Restore.
  2. Tap Restore from Backup and choose a previously exported .json file.
  3. Confirm. Restoring replaces the current data on the device with the contents of the backup file.
How do I export my data?

You can export your data in two formats:

  • JSON backup — a complete snapshot for backing up and restoring (Settings → Backup & Restore → Export Backup).
  • CSV export — spreadsheet-friendly files for doses, inventory, symptoms, weight, and protocols, bundled as a single export you can open in Numbers or Excel.

Both options use the standard iOS share sheet so you can save to Files, iCloud Drive, or email.

How do I delete my account or data?

Peptide Pilot has no account — your information never leaves your device, so there is no account to delete. To remove your data:

  • Delete everything: Use the data-reset option in Settings, or simply delete the app from your iPhone. Removing the app erases all locally stored data permanently.
  • Delete a single item: Swipe left on any vial, dose, or log entry and tap Delete.

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone unless you have an exported backup, so consider exporting your data first.

Troubleshooting

  • Force-quit and reopen the app: swipe up from the bottom of the screen and swipe the app away.
  • Make sure you're running the latest version of Peptide Pilot from the App Store.
  • Restart your iPhone if the app is slow or unresponsive.
  • If reminders aren't appearing, check Settings → Notifications → Peptide Pilot and confirm notifications are allowed.
  • If a calculation looks off, double-check the vial's total amount (mg) and the water volume (mL) you entered.
  • If the problem persists, export a backup, then reinstall the app and restore from that backup.
